About

A River Database, designed by anglers, for anglers. If you are new to the area of County Kerry, or live in the Kingdom, this is the place to come to find the latest information about the local fishing rivers in Kerry.

Please explore this web application and feel free to leave a review on a particular river that you visited. Please note, that the river you want to review must have a record in our database, so if no record exists, please add that particular river to our database.
